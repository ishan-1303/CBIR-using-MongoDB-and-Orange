Inception-v3

  • Inception-v3 is a pre-trained convolutional neural network model that is 48 layers deep

  • It is a widely-used image recognition model that has been shown to attain greater than 78.1% accuracy on the ImageNet dataset and around 93.9% accuracy in top 5 results.

  • The model is the culmination of many ideas introduced by multiple researchers over the past years.

  • Inception-v3 is the open source algorithm made by Google and used for objects classification.

  • It is a rethinking for the initial structure of computer vision after Inception-v1, Inception-v2 in 2015.
